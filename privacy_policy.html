Privacy Pledge

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

Electrode Repairs (“Electrode Repairs,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through our website and in connection with our repair, buy, sell, and customer service activities.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, when we may share it, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use our website or contact us, we may collect:

  • your name
  • phone number
  • email address
  • device details you submit through forms or quote requests
  • message content you send through contact, buy, sell, or quote forms
  • basic technical information such as browser type, device type, IP address, and pages visited through analytics, logs, embedded services, or security tools used on the site

2. How We Use Information

We may use your information to:

  • respond to inquiries and customer messages
  • provide quotes or follow up on repair, buy, or sell requests
  • communicate about requested services
  • maintain and improve our website
  • protect the security and integrity of our website and systems
  • comply with legal obligations
  • review website traffic and usage patterns to improve performance, content, and usability

3. Device Privacy and Customer Data

When you bring a device to Electrode Repairs for repair, upgrade, diagnostics, or transfer services, we aim to limit access to customer data to only what is reasonably necessary to perform requested services.

We do not access, copy, use, or share customer content except when access is reasonably necessary to diagnose, test, transfer, or complete requested work, or when otherwise authorized by the customer or required by law.

Customers are encouraged to back up their devices and remove or secure sensitive information before service whenever possible.

4. Website Forms and Quote Requests

If you submit information through our website’s contact, repair, buy, sell, or quote forms, we may receive and store the information you provide in order to respond to your request.

Some form or quote submissions may be processed through a WordPress-based backend, hosting environment, email delivery system, or related service providers used to receive and manage customer inquiries. As a result, your submitted information may pass through or be stored in those systems as part of normal website and business operations.

5. Cookies, Analytics, and Website Tools

Our website may use cookies, analytics tools, server logs, or similar technologies to understand traffic, improve performance, and protect site security. These tools may collect technical information such as browser type, IP address, referring pages, site interactions, and related diagnostic information.

We do not use customer information for targeted advertising unless specifically stated otherwise.

6. Third-Party Services, Maps, and External Links

Our website may include links to or integrations with third-party services, including WordPress-connected form handling, Google Maps embeds, map links, social media pages, and other external tools or services.

For example:

  • an embedded Google Map may load content directly from Google;
  • clicking our address may send you to Google Maps;
  • a submitted form may be routed through a hosted backend or email-processing system.

These third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies and practices, and Electrode Repairs is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party providers.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information for profit.

We may share information only as reasonably necessary with:

  • service providers or vendors who help operate our website or communications
  • hosting, form, email, analytics, security, or technical support providers
  • legal authorities or other parties when required by law or when reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or property

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for customer service, recordkeeping, website operations, legal compliance, and related business purposes.

Information submitted through website forms may be retained in email systems, hosting systems, WordPress-related systems, or other business tools used to receive and manage customer requests.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information and customer devices. However, no method of electronic transmission, storage, or website operation can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the website.

11. Optional SMS Communications Notice

Electrode Repairs does not currently represent through this Privacy Policy that it operates an active SMS marketing program unless and until such a program is actually launched.

If Electrode Repairs later offers SMS or text-message communications, any such program may involve collecting and using your mobile number and message-related records to send service updates, customer support messages, or marketing texts, depending on your consent and the nature of the program. Any future SMS marketing program should be accompanied by clear opt-in language, applicable terms, and legally compliant consent practices.

12. Your Choices

You may contact us to:

  • ask questions about this Privacy Policy
  • request updates to inaccurate contact information you submitted
  • ask us to review or delete website-submitted information where appropriate and subject to legal or operational limits

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
