About Electrode Repairs

Built on trust, quality work, and reliable service.

From a one-person startup to a full team, our focus has stayed the same: honest communication, dependable repairs, and a better experience for every customer.

Our Story

Electrode Repairs began in August of 2023 during a transition period, taking over a location that had seen a variety of businesses come and go over the years. While the space itself was not new to change, the goal from day one was clear: build something lasting, grounded in trust and reliable quality.

The early months were focused on establishing that identity. Much of the initial traffic came from customers expecting the previous repair shop, which meant every interaction mattered in defining what Electrode Repairs would become. Growth came gradually, built through consistency, problem-solving, and a commitment to doing things the right way.

What started as a one-person operation has since grown into a full team, with dedicated roles across repair, administration, and marketing. As the business expanded, so did its presence in the community. Electrode Repairs is an active member of the Chamber of Commerce and the Better Business Bureau, regularly sponsors local events, and offers free e-waste recycling as a way to give back and reduce unnecessary waste.

Today, the business continues to evolve, but the foundation remains the same: earn trust, deliver quality work, and build something that stands on its own.

Mission Statement

Electrode Repairs exists to make electronics repair simple, honest, and trustworthy. We believe quality work and clear communication should be the standard, not the exception.

Our mission is to ensure every customer leaves confident in the repair, relieved by the experience, and comfortable knowing they have found a shop they can rely on.

We are proud to be part of our local community and committed to supporting it through involvement, service, and hands-on training opportunities for interns and future technicians.

Our Warranty

You can rest easy when you have your device repaired with us. We offer a 90-day hassle-free warranty on all our repairs and a 30-day warranty on all devices we have for sale.

If something is out of the ordinary after your repair or purchase, rest assured that we will take care of it for you free of charge. Restrictions may apply.
